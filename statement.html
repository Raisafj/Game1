Column C: .The game will not only tell a story but also the emotion the character is feeling and within that story he has to follow a series of rules to reach his goal which is to reach his date. Anthropy argues that “The experience we call a game is created by the interactions between different rules, but the rules themselves aren’t the game, the interaction is! A game can’t exist without a player or players: someone needs to be engaging with the rules for the experience to happen.” Hence, the rules are not the game but TimTim’s interaction with the rules is what makes it a game.

Column D: We took page-Oriented approach with 10 pages. .

Artist's Statement:

TimTim and his first date is an adventurous browser-game, built with HTML/CSS and JavaScript. We are taking a story-based game approach where the player TimTim) needs to reach his first date before she leaves the restaurant. To reach her, he has to complete a series of tasks, if not, she leaves and TimTim’s first date is ruined, and loses the game.
